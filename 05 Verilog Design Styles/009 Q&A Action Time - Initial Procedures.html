Why am I getting sum=x?

(thanks to Muddada Prasanth)

module procedures();
reg [7:0] a,b;
reg [15:0] prod;
wire [8:0] sum;

assign sum=a+b;

always @(a or b) begin
    prod=a*b;
end

always @(a) begin
    $display ("a=%0d",a);
end

always @(*) begin
    $display("a=%0d,b=%0d,sum=%0d,prod=%0d", a,b,sum,prod);
end

initial begin
    #1; a=10;b=99;
    #1; b=33;
    #1; a=b<<2;
    #1; b=b>>3;
    #1; b=b+1;
end
endmodule


The a, b, prod, sum have the value X (unknown) until 1ns because a=10; b=99 is executed after 1ns.

Until that time the default values of all variables is X (unknown) by default.
