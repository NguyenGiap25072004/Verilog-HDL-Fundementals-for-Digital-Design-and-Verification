Why do we create sim folder in action time folder?what is it necessity?

(thanks to Raj Lahari)


We create another folder because Modelsim generates several files for each project. In this way we keep separate what Modelsim generates from our own Verilog files.


It is also an industry best-practice to have different folders inside a project and to group there files of the same type:

  • 1 folder for RTL where you keep the design files

  • 1 folder for verification where you keep the tesbench and tests

  • 1 folder for simulation, etc.


The above structure may vary from project to project, company to company, but at the end of the day it is a way to keep your files organized and easy to find. This becomes critical when your project has thousands of files or even more.
