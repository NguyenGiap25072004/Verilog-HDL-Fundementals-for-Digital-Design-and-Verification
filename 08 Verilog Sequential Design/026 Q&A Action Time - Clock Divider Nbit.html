Here is how the synthesis results looks like for the downloadable code.

The divide by 2 is only a D flip-flop that has the Q inverted and connected with D (it has that small bubble before D).


The counter has 4 bits and is composed from 4x D flip flops and an adder (similar with the drawing from the course). The 4bits counter outputs contain the input frequency divided by 2/4/8/16.

If you change the size of the parameter you can obtain a smaller or a bigger frequency divider.
